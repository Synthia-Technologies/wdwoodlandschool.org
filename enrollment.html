------ Daily Schedule ------

Time Activities
7:00-7:30 Staff Time: Opening Preparations
7:30-8:30 Arrival, Transition Activities, and Breakfast
8:30-8:55 Community Circle and Launch to talk about news, share stories, and learn about the day's activities
8:55-9:15 Clean-Up and Bathroom Break
9:15-10:15 Woodland Excursions in 2 age groups (2 ½ to 3 ½ and 3 ½ to 5 ½)
10:15-10:45 Woodland Excursions and Discorvery Trecks; Building structures and making outdoor creations, collecting, and recording
10:45-11:00 Bathroom Break and Transition Activities
11:00-11:40 Lunch (Outdoors some days, weather permitting)
11:40-12:30 Creating Documentation and Narrative Descriptions for observations, outdoor creations, recordings/photos,and/or collected artifacts
12:30-2:00 Nap time / Quiet time
2:00-2:15 Wake up; Bathroom Break; Transition Activities
2:15-2:45 Snack and Clean-Up
2:45-3:30 Art Explorations related to Outdoor Experiences (outdoors or indoors)
3:30-4:15 Free Choice: Engage in Music, Drama, Creative Movement, Storytelling (Dictation) and/or Construction Projects (outdoors or indoors)
4:15-4:35 Reflection and Closing Circle: Review of Day (actions, achievements, process, and lingering questions) and Teasers or Questions for the Next Day
4:35-5:00 Parent Arrival; Transition Activities: Quiet time activities, Exploration of artifacts, and Book browsing
5:00-5:30 Staff Time: Additional Cleaning and Closing Procedures

------ Basic Tuition & Fee Schedule ------

Full-Time = 5 days per week-Monday through Friday $250/wk
Part-Time = 2 days per week-Monday and Wednesday $120/wk
or Tuesday and Thursday $120/wk

Fee: Annual fee of $50 for supplies - due prior to initial enrollment and on enrollment anniversary

Tuition is due weekly before the first day of attendance. Payment is for enrollment, so tuition is due regardless of attendance.

* Some modification may be possible with Director approval.
